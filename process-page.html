Our cleaning process

Simple, transparent, and effective — here’s how we work from booking to sparkling results.

How it works

Booking professional cleaning has never been easier.

Step
Booking made simple

You choose the service and time that fit your schedule — online or by phone. We confirm instantly and prepare everything for a smooth experience.

Step
On-time arrival

Our uniformed team arrives right on time, equipped with all professional tools and eco-friendly products. Punctuality and respect for your home are our standards.

Step
Deep eco-cleaning

We use safe, non-toxic products and advanced equipment to remove dust, stains, and allergens. Each surface is treated carefully to restore its original look and freshness.

Step
Final inspection

Before we finish, we inspect every detail together with you to make sure everything meets the highest quality standards.

Step
Enjoy a spotless home

Relax and enjoy a perfectly clean, fresh, and safe home — ready for your family, guests, and comfort.
